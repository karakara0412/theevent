Our tagline is “new party, new beginnings,” but that’s not just a catchphrase: this year we’ll be in a new hotel just off Fremont Street (aka the perfect place to sneak away to for the obligatory Facebook friendly vacation photos).

School Detention Hosted by Princess Kelley

*Pre-registration required. Limited to ~40 people. Bottoms and switches (playing as bottoms) of any gender identity welcome.

We're trying something new, and we're really excited about it! This event is designed to be headspace intensive. Unlike other school events (including ones that I run and love!), this is less about having a "fun" time with friends and getting up to shenanigans, and more about the headspace of being at school and being in trouble. (It's still going to be fun! Just different fun!). Remember, this is a "Saturday school/detention" event, so you've already done something to land yourself here in the first place! No need to act up for attention.

If that doesn't sound like something you're interested in or will be able to stick to, please do not register for this event.

Overall format/vibe:

It's Saturday school. You've done something to land yourself here, and you and all the others in detention today are going to be facing some consequences. You will spend your time working on assigments-- either classwork you missed, or writing lines or essays about your misbehaviors. And you will also likely be visiting with one of the resident disciplinarians of the school, and will face corporal punishment. One by one, each student will be called into a side room where they will be punished and then sent back to continue working silently on their assignment. Punishments are in a semi private room (there will be 2 disciplinarians and thus 2 students per room), but it's likely that some things will be heard by the rest of people in detention... waiting is so often the hardest part, isn't it?

Punishments will be handled by one of 4 disciplinarians (at least one male, one female). We are not able to honor requests for a specific person, but can honor requests for a specific gender. You may select your level of undress, intensity, and implement in the sign up. Punishments will last around 5 minutes. The entire event will be around an hour.

The class will be monitored by Miss Kelley and an additional authority figure. They are there to grade papers and keep things in general order. They should not be having to deal with shenanigans (seriously). First offense of talking out of turn, or general misbehavior will be dealt with on the spot, either with corporal punishment, or, more likely, cornertime. A second offense and you will be asked to leave the event. Remember, the point of this event is the nervous waiting headspace, so if someone is being silly and acting up, it ruins it for those who are trying to stay in the headspace. If you misbehave in class, you may be punished by either the male or female supervisor for on the spot punishments, and thus if you have a firm gender preference for your play, definitely don't act up.

You are not required to see a disciplinairan and get spanked to participate in this event. You can just attend the "school!" If you choose to not see a disciplinarian, you also will not be given any on the spot physical punishments, but as with everyone else, will receive one warning and then be asked to leave the event if you become a disruption.

Dress code: You may wear whatever you like as long as it fits the idea of 'high school.' That can be a school uniform if you want (not outlandishly slutty ones please), but jeans and a t-shirt, cheerleading outfit, sports uniform, dresses, pants, whatever you actually wore in high school, etc. is more what we're expecting. We aren't going to be like the actual terrible schools that say no tanktops becasue they'll distract the boys, but we will remind that this is supposed to feel "real" rather than costumed, and is co-ed. Think Breakfast Club-- but less 80s! The dresscode should not cause you stress. Just show up in clothes.

We are genuinely so excited about this event and think it has the potential to be properly magical. We hope you enjoy it as well! Can't wait to see you there!
